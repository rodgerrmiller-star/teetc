About Telami Etham College

Discover our history, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 2010, Telami Etham Teachers' and Business Studies Training Institution has grown from a small training center to a premier college in Nairobi, Kenya. Our name "Telami Etham" reflects our commitment to excellence in education and character building.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and market-driven teacher training and business education that equips students with practical skills, theoretical knowledge, and ethical values for professional success.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in teacher training and business education in Kenya, recognized for producing competent, innovative, and ethical professionals who contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Student-centered approach
  • Community engagement
